Sample 1

Stay calm and immediately evacuate the apartment. Don’t use elevators—take the stairs. Close doors behind you to slow the fire’s spread. Call 911 once you’re safely outside.

Sample 2

If smoke is heavy, crawl low to the ground to avoid inhaling toxic fumes. Cover your nose with a cloth. Head to the nearest exit and don’t stop for belongings.

Sample 3

If trapped, seal doors with towels to block smoke. Signal for help at a window. Stay near the floor where the air is cleaner, and wait for firefighters to rescue you.
